About Me

Find out a bit about me and how I made it to where I am now

Introduction

With nearly a decades worth of experience in graphic & web design as well as e-commerce and digital marketing, I have lot of interest in building interactive and functional websites that will enable businesses to grow in the ever changing digital world we now live in.

I have come a long way since I was a student studying Computer Games Design at University. Once I graduated in 2012 I started working for Mama's and Papa's, a medium sized, UK based baby retailer. It was here where my interest in the world of web started, doing e-commerce and web design. Since then I have been lucky enough to work for a variety of different companies, from a global Scientific retailer to a small family run design business. This has helped me gain huge amounts of experience in the field with HTML, CSS and a variety of different CMS's.

Why Choose me?

The best answer I can give is why not? I would like to try to help business grow with my experience, and can offer a cheap alternative to big, faceless businesses that exist. Offering a more hands on experience, I can walk you through the design and creation process and build you something we can all be proud of.

In recent years, there has been a big push on "do it yourself" websites, but there are so many platforms to choose from with so many things on offer, so it's a mine field to even know where to begin sometimes. There are also a lot of essential things that these companies don't tell you to help and improve your online pressence, so I would like to offer an affordable service that provides you with everything you need.
